# End-User Onboarding Guide

## Installation

This app is not in the App Store or Google Play. Install it from the web URL (GitHub Pages when deployed).

### iOS (Safari)

1. Open the app URL in Safari
2. Tap Share → **Add to Home Screen**
3. Confirm the name and tap **Add**

### Android (Chrome)

1. Open the app URL in Chrome
2. Tap Menu (⋮) → **Install App** or **Add to Home Screen**
3. Confirm

## On-the-Water Workflow

1. **Launch** the app from your home screen
2. **Capture GPS** — tap the large teal button as soon as you have a fish alongside. Or tap a map grid cell if you know the area visually
3. **Fill the form** — tag type, tag number, species, length, condition, location name
4. **Photo** — tap the photo area; use camera or gallery
5. **Save Catch** — form resets for the next fish

## Sync at the Dock

1. Connect to Wi-Fi or cellular
2. Open the app → **Sync** tab
3. Review the unsent count
4. Tap **Submit Sync to DNR**
5. Wait for the progress bar and success message

## Backup Export

If sync fails:

1. Tap **Download Backup ZIP**
2. Email the ZIP file to the program leader
3. Optionally clear exported records when prompted

## Tips

- GPS works without map tiles when offline
- CR tags: 5 digits, minimum 10" fish
- K tags: 6 digits, minimum 18" fish
- Spotted Seatrout shows a **DO NOT TAG** warning
